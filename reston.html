Reston, VA

Acceptable Recyclables

- Aluminum cans
- Shampoo bottles
- Cereal boxes
- Jelly jars
- Newspapers
- All Valley Yellow Pages

Not Acceptable

- Motor oil
- Batteries
- Pesticides
- Paint
- Paint thinners
- Electronics (such as television, computers, cell phones, video game consoles, DVD players)
- Household cleaners
- Takeout containers
- Plastic bags
- Diapers
- Pizza boxes
- Styrofoam
- Packaging peanuts
- Clamshell containers either Styrofoam or clear plastic

Acceptable Plastics

- You can recycle your plastic bags in grocery stores.
- Plastic food and beverage bottles, jugs, tubs
