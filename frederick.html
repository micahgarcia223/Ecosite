Frederick, Maryland

Acceptable

Ferrous metals
Aluminum, copper, stainless steel, and brass
Clean corrugated cardboard
Empty food and beverage metal cans
Empty glass bottles and jars
Clean paper, cartons, and cardboard
Empty plastic bottles, tubs, jars, and jugs
PC and other circuit boards

Not acceptable

Plastic bags
Shredded paper
Hoses, cords, and chains
Soiled paper or cardboard(such as pizza boxes)
Food wrappers, box liners, and chip bags
Produce pins and takeout containers
Paper towels, napkins, or wet/dirty paper
Tanks or scrap metal
Tires
Garbage, rags, paper, or other debris

Things to keep in mind

Materials must be loose.
Putting items that are not allowed in the recycle bin harms the program.
For more information, go to the Reliable Recycling Center website and the Frederick County Government website.
