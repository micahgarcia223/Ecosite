NOTICE

As of April 25th, 2019, residents are asked to not place glass into the single stream recycling bins, the local centers are not accepting them as a recyclable.

Acceptable Paper

Cardboard (no size restrictions)
Mixed Paper: Newspaper, magazines, catalogs, white & colored paper, junk mail, phone books, hard back books, pizza boxes, paperboard (cereal/cracker boxes, paper towel rolls, soda boxes, etc.), and junk mail including all envelopes. Flattened Cardboard

Acceptable Metals

Aluminum cans
Metal household items (large household appliances, bicycles, cabinets, doors, grills, iron furniture, etc.)
Metal Cans

Acceptable Plastics

Plastics 1,2,3,5,7
Water Bottles
Soap containers

Unacceptable items for recycling

Styrofoam and Packing Peanuts
Plastic bags
Pots and Pans

Helpful Notes!

Recyclables should be clean & empty.
Do not bag recyclables. Leave items loose in bin.
Empty & flatten cardboard boxes.
single stream recycling services do not accept plastic bags, but you can take them to your local grocery store and dispose of them in their special bins.
Going green doesn't stop at recycling!Reduce your single-use product use to lessen your wasteload.
